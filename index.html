Leading the structural development, hardware integration, and testing of student-built rockets.

Leadership • Systems Integration • Testing
```

As Head of Rocketry Structures at Eirspace, I led the development and integration of the rocket's structural systems, taking responsibility for turning designs into functioning hardware. My role involved coordinating the interfaces between different subsystems, ensuring components could be manufactured and assembled effectively, and supporting the team throughout the integration process.

A major focus of the project was hardware testing and validation. Rather than treating the design as purely a CAD exercise, we continually tested assumptions through assembly, fit checks, and iterative improvements. This process required balancing structural requirements, manufacturability, and reliability while working closely with members responsible for avionics, propulsion, and recovery systems.

The experience strengthened my understanding of systems engineering and reinforced the importance of careful testing in developing reliable hardware. Leading the structures team also developed my ability to manage technical work, communicate design decisions, and take ownership of complex engineering challenges from initial design through integration and testing.
```
